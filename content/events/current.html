Community Over Code Asia 2025 ¶

Applications are open now! And will close on the 9th May 2025. Apply as early in the process as you are able - even if you are a potential speaker and do not know if you will be accepted, we advise you to apply anyway, you can always cancel later on if need be. Also note that being a Speaker does not imply success but is one of many criteria.
